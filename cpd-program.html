CPD

EDUCATIONAL LEADERSHIP COURSE 50 CPD HOURS

This course is designed for Leadership & Management Professionals (Any Organization), Strategic Planners, Change Managers, Action Leaders, Directors, Educational Leaders, Principals and Heads, School Coordinators , Experienced Teachers & Professionals, Subject Specialists, Entrepreneurs, Freelancers to engage them in a transformational process of Leadership via problem-solving and decision making.

ECED LEADERSHIP COURSE 64 CPD HOURS

This course is developed for Early Years Principals, EY Heads, EY Teachers, Assistant Teachers, Montessori Directress, Kindergarten Teachers, Subject Specialists, Authors, Trainers & Early Years Practitioners. It aims to create awareness to a trainee teacher with the fundamentals of child development in Early years. It provides theoretical and practical knowledge and understanding the principles of Early Childhood education based on child development.

CURRICULUM DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT COURSE (150 CPD HOURS)

This course is intended for Curriculum developers & designers, Instructional Coaches & designers, Subject Matter Experts, Principals, Academicians, Master Trainers, Educationists & Experienced Educators.

TEACHER LEADER COURSE 50 CPD HOURS

This course is developed for Primary Secondary & Higher secondary Teachers & Heads. The course aims to create awareness to a trainee teacher with the fundamentals of teaching as a Teacher Leader in Primary and secondary Years. It provides theoretical and practical knowledge and understanding the principles of differentiated teachings, Activity-based learning, lesson planning with effective strategies of assessment and evaluation.
